Our Heritage

Nepal's Most
Beloved Hotel

Since 1996, Hotel Dream Palace has been the jewel of Bhairahawa — where world-class luxury harmonizes with authentic Nepali warmth. Located at Siddharthanagar-8, Ravi Path, we are the premier gateway for pilgrims visiting Lumbini, the sacred birthplace of Lord Buddha, and for travelers seeking unmatched comfort in southern Nepal.

  • Prime Lumbini Gateway LocationJust 20 minutes from Lumbini — the sacred birthplace of Lord Buddha. Perfect for pilgrims & cultural travelers.
  • World-Class FacilitiesAir-conditioned rooms, multi-cuisine restaurant, conference halls, banquet venue, and 24/7 concierge.
  • Authentic Nepali HospitalityPersonalized, genuine, and unforgettable service rooted in Nepali warmth and tradition.
